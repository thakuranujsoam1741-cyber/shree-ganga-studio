ABOUT
SHREEGANGASTUDIO

Shashankimages is run by a dynamic team of creative professionals with experience in wedding photography & cinematography based out of New Delhi, India.

Each of our team members is a unique & amazing individual, absolutely beautiful in their own way. Over the years, we have built our team with a lot of love and care, and skill. This allows us to easily navigate between roles & adapt to a situation quickly, so we can capture what is authentic & real. We have a mix of full-time team members and long-term freelancers who we have carefully identified over years, so we are able to service intimate as well as large scale weddings with equal ease.
